Directions

From U.S. 101

  • Take exit to Page Mill Rd/Oregon Expressway
  • Turn right on El Camino Real
  • Take a left onto Palm Dr
  • Take right onto Campus Drive
  • Continue to Panama St and take a left
  • Parking Structure 2 will be on your left; Durand will be on your left two blocks further down the road.

From U.S. 280

  • Take exit to Sand Hill Rd
  • Turn left onto Alpine Rd
  • Take first left onto Junipero Serra Blvd
  • Follow to Campus Drive, take a left
  • Take right onto Panama St.
  • Parking Structure 2 will be on your left; Durand will be on your left two blocks further down the road.
