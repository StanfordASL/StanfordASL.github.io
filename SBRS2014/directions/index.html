Driving

The symposium will take place in McCaw Hall located within the Arrillaga Alumni Center. The hall is on the left-hand side of the building from the main entrance. Detailed driving instructions may be found at the alumni center website or through Google Maps.

Public Transportation

The Arrillaga Alumni Center is accessible from the Palo Alto Caltrain Station by way of the free Stanford Marguerite shuttle system. Scheduling details may be found at the Marguerite website; Line Y should be the best choice.
