Lunch will not be provided at the symposium, but there will be coffee and pastries available at registration and for the breaks throughout the day. We recommend The Arbuckle Dining Pavillion around the corner from the Arrillaga Alumni Center as the simplest dining option during the lunch break (see the map below). The Sports Cafe and Coupa Cafe are also close. For attendees that fancy a longer walk, an interactive map of the array of campus dining options may be found here; in particular Tresidder Memorial Union features a wide variety of restaurants at one location.
