Driving

The symposium will take place in Paul Brest Hall at Stanford University. Detailed driving instructions may be found here or through Google Maps.

Public Transportation

Paul Brest Hall is accessible from the Palo Alto Caltrain Station by way of the free Stanford Marguerite shuttle system. Scheduling details may be found at the Marguerite website; Line Y should be the best choice.
