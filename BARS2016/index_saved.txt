<div style="font-size:.9em" class="content">
The 2016 Bay Area Robotics Symposium aims to bring together roboticists from the Bay Area. The program will consist of a mix of faculty, student and industry presentations. For more details see the event <a href="schedule/">schedule</a>.

<hr>

<b> Registration is free for students and $20 for non-students. Early registration prices expire October 9. Registration is capped at 475 so be sure to get your ticket ASAP at <a href="https://www.eventbrite.com/e/sbrs-2014-registration-12814760293">BARS 2016 Eventbrite</a></b>.
<br>
The <a href="schedule/">program</a> will feature:
<ul>
<li> Presentations from Stanford and Berkeley faculty </li>
<li> Talks from bay area industry representatives </li>
<li> Keynote talks (TBD)
<li> Student poster session
</ul>

<hr>
Organizers: <a href="http://web.stanford.edu/~pavone/">Marco Pavone</a> (Stanford) and <a href="https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~anca/">Anca Dragan</a> (UC Berkeley)
<br>
Local arrangements: <a href="http://web.stanford.edu/~pavone/">Marco Pavone</a> and <a href="http://asl.stanford.edu/people/ben-hockman/">Ben Hockman</a> (Stanford)
