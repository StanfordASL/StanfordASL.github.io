Driving

The symposium will take place in Hauck Auditorium in David and Joan Traitel Building at Hoover Institution, Stanford University. Detailed driving instructions may be found here or through Google Maps.

Public Transportation

Hauck Auditorium is accessible from the Palo Alto Caltrain Station by way of the free Stanford Marguerite shuttle system. Scheduling details may be found at the Marguerite website; Line P should be the best choice.
